About Me

I am a Punjabi Digital Artist from Canada. I've been doing digital art for around 3 years, since the pandemics beginning. I picked up digital art as a hobby to express my love for my favorite series. For context, Adventure Time is one of my favorite shows and it was the main reason to why I learned digital art. I'd constantly see amazing artwork of the characters and it had peeked my interest. I now draw my own characters (OC), album covers, my favorite shows and games as well as commission work.
